Privacy Policy for buckets.nosecreekweb.ca

The privacy policy is simple: we don't store any of your personal data. This app will request that you log in using Google or Dropbox and choose a .buckets file. You will need to grant this app read-only access to your Google Drive or Dropbox file. This is only so we can display your budget info to you. We don't send this data to any servers. A copy of some of the data will be stored only in your own browser's local storage.

We may use Goat Counter to collect some basic information about how many visitors visit this site, but we have chosen to use Goat Counter because it does not collect any personal information. The Goat Counter privacy policy can be viewed here. This is the only information collected by Goat Counter: URL of the visited page. – Referer header. – User-Agent header. – Screen size. – Country and region name based on IP address. – A hash of the IP address, User-Agent, and random number
