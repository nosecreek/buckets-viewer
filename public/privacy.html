Privacy Policy

The privacy policy is simple: we don't store any of your personal data. This app will request that you log in using Google and choose a .buckets file. This is only so we can display your budget info to you. We don't store or send this data to any servers. A copy of some of the data will be stored only in your own browser's local storage.

We may use Goat Counter to collect some basic information about how many visitors visit this site, but we have chosen to use Goat Counter because it does not collect any personal information. The Goat Counter privacy policy can be viewed here.
